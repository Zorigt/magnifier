  • The app.VERB() methods provide the routing functionality in Express,
  • where VERB is one of the HTTP verbs, such as app.post(). Multiple
  • callbacks may be given, all are treated equally, and behave just
  • like middleware, with the one exception that these callbacks may invoke
  • next('route') to bypass the remaining route callback(s). This mechanism
  • can be used to perform pre-conditions on a route then pass control to
  • subsequent routes when there is no reason to proceed with the route
  • matched.
